# Source Whitelist

This file defines the **only authoritative sources** that the system is allowed to use
for answering Slovak tax, accounting, payroll, and contribution-related questions.

If information is not supported by these sources, the system must respond with
**“Insufficient data” or “Ambiguous”**.

---

## Laws

### Slov-Lex (Zbierka zákonov SR)
- **URL:** https://www.slov-lex.sk/
- **Institution:** Ministerstvo spravodlivosti SR
- **Authority:** Official Collection of Laws of the Slovak Republic
- **Scope:** Binding Slovak legislation, including consolidated versions and amendments
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18

---

## Tax Administration

### Finančná správa Slovenskej republiky
- **URL:** https://www.financnasprava.sk/
- **Institution:** Finančné riaditeľstvo SR (under Ministry of Finance SR)
- **Authority:** Official tax and customs authority
- **Scope:** Tax guidance, methodology, official interpretations, and e-services  
  (VAT / DPH, personal and corporate income tax, customs)
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18

---

## Social Insurance

### Sociálna poisťovňa
- **URL:** https://www.socpoist.sk/
- **Institution:** Sociálna poisťovňa
- **Authority:** Public-law social insurance institution
- **Scope:** Mandatory social insurance schemes, contribution obligations, benefits,
  and related legislation
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18

---

## Health Insurance & Contributions

### Úrad pre dohľad nad zdravotnou starostlivosťou (ÚDZS)
- **URL:** https://www.udzs-sk.sk/
- **Institution:** Úrad pre dohľad nad zdravotnou starostlivosťou
- **Authority:** Supervisory authority for public health insurance
- **Scope:** Oversight of health insurance companies and administration of public
  health insurance contributions
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18

---

## Public Health Regulation

### Úrad verejného zdravotníctva SR (ÚVZ SR)
- **URL:** https://www.uvzsr.sk/
- **Institution:** Úrad verejného zdravotníctva SR (under Ministry of Health SR)
- **Authority:** National public health authority
- **Scope:** Binding public health regulations and guidelines
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18

---

## Financial Policy

### Ministerstvo financií Slovenskej republiky (MF SR)
- **URL:** https://www.mfsr.sk/
- **Institution:** Ministerstvo financií SR
- **Authority:** Central government financial authority
- **Scope:** Tax legislation, financial policy, budget, and oversight of financial
  administration
- **Accessed:** 2025-12-18
